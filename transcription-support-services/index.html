Transcription Support
Global Healthcare Billing Partner provides reliable, accurate and professional medical transcription services to healthcare facilities in various specialities. Both insurance companies and healthcare facilities require digitized electronic records as well as transcription at all levels. Global Healthcare Billing Partner’ transcription services halve operational costs and their accurate medical transcripts expedite the entire revenue cycle management of any healthcare facility.
The Global Healthcare Billing Partner team is composed of transcription specialists, proof-readers and quality analysts who work along with subject matter experts. To ensure an accurate transcription our team begins by deciphering the spoken words of physicians with contextual understanding, followed by accelerated EMR implementation. We have helped smoothen the transition process involved in EMR implementation for numerous facilities.
All documentation is TAT-guaranteed and 100% HIPAA compliant. A robust training program offered to employees, continued on-job training, coupled with internal reviews, multiple levels of quality checks for all documentation ensure the highest quality. Positive feedback received from clients have encouraged us to continue expanding our team and enabled us to become one of the best providers of transcription support services.
